## 🤖 Response

### English
Yes. We focus specifically on Egyptian and regional e-commerce: marketplace behavior, COD challenges, logistics realities, and how Egyptian buyers actually shop on Amazon, Noon, Jumia, Talabat, and Raneen. Our whole model is built around **direct sales growth**, not just “branding campaigns”. [ad-startup.com](https://www.ad-startup.com/home)

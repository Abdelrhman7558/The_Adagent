## 🤖 Response

### English
Here’s a typical pattern we see:  
 Month 1: Setup, listings, and test campaigns → first consistent sales.  
 Month 2: Optimizing ads, fixing listings, adjusting prices → higher ROAS and more stable orders.  
 Month 3: Scaling the winning products and campaigns → noticeable jump in total marketplace sales.

 شهر ٣: تكبير المنتجات والحملات الكسبانة → قفزة واضحة في إجمالي مبيعات الماركت بليس.

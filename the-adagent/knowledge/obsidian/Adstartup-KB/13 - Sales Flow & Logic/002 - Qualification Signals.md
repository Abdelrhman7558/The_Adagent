# 🚦 Qualification Signals

## 🟢 Green Flags

| Signal | Weight |
|--------|--------|
| Own brand | 🔥 High |
| 10+ SKUs | 🔥 High |
| Margin > 25% | 🔥 High |
| Inventory ready | 🔥 High |
| Wants to start now | 🔥 High |
| Decision maker in chat | ✅ Medium |

## 🔴 Red Flags

| Signal | Weight |
|--------|--------|
| No physical product | ❌ Instant DQ |
| Dropshipping only | ❌ Instant DQ |
| Margin < 15% | ❌ High risk |
| No ads budget | ⚠️ Warning |
| "Let me think" × 3 | ⚠️ Stalling |
